Voxel-Space Renderer

This demo is based on the Voxel-Space renderer as used in the Comanche series of games from the 90's. The engine has been reverse-engineered by Sebastian Macke (https://www.simulationcorner.net/) and the map files can be found on his github. The method used here is pretty much identical to the one on his website, just with added 3D camera controls.

The imperfections become noticeable when you start looking too closely but, considering how simple the algorithm is, the result is fairly impressive. One main flaw is that vertical lines remain vertical on the screen as you tilt the camera up/down, rather than splaying in/out as you would expect from the change in perspective. See the steps on the temple in level 1, looking down at them should cause the bottoms to become narrower and the tops wider, and looking up the opposite.
